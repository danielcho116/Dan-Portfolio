Hi, my name is Daniel. I’m an industrial design graduate from the Rhode Island School of Design, currently situated in New York.

I currently work as an industrial designer at Baum Essex and design functional domestic objects with a goal of enriching people’s everyday experiences.

Previously, I’ve worked at Jacob Jensen Design, a design studio of the late Danish Industrial designer, where I was ingrained with the iterative methods and the characteristic design language of functional minimalism that is rooted in Nordic values.

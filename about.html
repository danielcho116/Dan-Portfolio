Hi, my name is Daniel. I earned my BFA in Industrial Design at Rhode Island School of Design in 2015 and currently situated in New York.

I currently work as an industrial designer at Baum Essex and design functional domestic objects in tabletop, kitchen, storage, and furniture categories, with a goal of enriching people’s everyday experiences.

Previously, I’ve worked at Jacob Jensen Design, a design studio of the late Danish Industrial designer, where I was ingrained with the iterative methods (intricate card stock and foam core prototyping) and the characteristic design language of functional minimalism that is rooted in Nordic values.
